Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour2 1/4 cups
Butter1 cup
Sugar3/4 cup
Brown sugar3/4 cup
Eggs2
Chocolate chips2 cups
Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
Step 2: Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
Step 3: Add eggs and vanilla.
Step 4: Add flour and chocolate chips.
Step 5: Bake for 10-12 minutes.
